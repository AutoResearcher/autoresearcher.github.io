Join Our Team

We're looking for exceptional researchers, engineers, and scientists who are passionate about accelerating scientific discovery through AI. At AutoResearcher, you'll work on cutting-edge technology that has the potential to transform how science is conducted.

Research Scientist, Autonomous Experimentation

San Francisco, CA or Remote

We're seeking research scientists with expertise in experimental design, reinforcement learning, and automated laboratory systems to expand our autonomous experimentation capabilities.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement algorithms for autonomous experimental design
  • Develop methods for efficient scientific exploration
  • Collaborate with domain scientists to validate approaches
  • Publish research at top-tier ML and scientific conferences

Requirements:

  • PhD in Computer Science, ML, or related field
  • Strong background in experimental design and ML
  • Experience with reinforcement learning and/or Bayesian optimization
  • Excellent programming skills in Python

ML Engineer, Scientific Language Models

San Francisco, CA or Remote

Join our team building the next generation of language models specifically designed to understand and generate scientific content.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and optimize large-scale language models for scientific domains
  • Create efficient training and inference pipelines
  • Implement techniques for knowledge extraction from scientific literature
  • Build evaluation frameworks for scientific language models

Requirements:

  • Experience with large language models
  • Strong software engineering skills
  • Knowledge of distributed computing and ML frameworks

Research Engineer, Multi-Modal Science Understanding

San Francisco, CA or Remote

Help us build models that can understand and interpret scientific data across multiple modalities, including text, images, and structured data.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop multi-modal models for scientific data understanding
  • Create methods for interpreting scientific figures and visualizations
  • Build systems for cross-modal information synthesis
  • Contribute to research publications and open-source projects

Requirements:

  • Experience with computer vision and NLP
  • Strong programming skills
  • Background in multi-modal ML techniques
